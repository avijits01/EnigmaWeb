We Are ENIGMA Ai Research

This department aims to delve deep into Deep Learning while keeping intuition as the priority. We wish to promote a project-driven approach to learning, which we believe is crucial for one's further endeavours. Often in such a strategy, you can expect to spend quite a lot of time and energy working on projects, which is unquestionably a much longer climb when you're just starting up all while having to balance your time at university entirely on your own.

So here we are, at Enigma, seeking to bridge this gap by bringing like-minded people together in a conducive environment. And this particular environment of Ai Research would involve working together on initiatives that prepare us better for our future from an understanding first perspective. In simpler words, you can view this as a hike and this initiative as our effort to set up a basecamp.
