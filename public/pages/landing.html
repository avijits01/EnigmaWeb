ENIGMA

The very first initiative of its kind, thrives on catapulting efforts of highly driven individuals towards realizing their projects, that will not only prime them for universities abroad and professional journey but make them an essential part of making our institution a more connected community than it has ever been.
JOIN US

WHAT DO WE DO HERE?

Working on valuable projects:


Our club has a strong focus on project-based learning, that consists of strengthening the fundamental concepts to then intuitively solve real-world problems

Working on technical and non-technical literature:


The club will have an ever-expanding domain. Brainstorming sessions and discussing new ideas will be a regular part of club activities. We believe expertise over a subject with true articulation creates souvenirs of creativity. We will not just learn skills but the process of creating them and voicing them out through regular events and activities.

OUR GOAL

A novel way of savoring your days at college!

Any kind of platform, that brings together people in collaborating to achieve common landmarks of learning objectives that also coincide with their interests is always a delight to be a part of as a student. On getting a glimpse of the diversity of interests our club aims to cater to, one would realize our club's holistic approach to learning. A diversified skillset can make learning more efficient and meaningfully fun.

Promoting startup oriented interdisciplinary learning!

New ideas will be formalized and channeled into Projects, ones that will have an interdisciplinary approach aimed to make an individual not just learn development but how to gather ideas and systematically channel them into products. We will learn about different established systems and how technological innovations can create and influence one.

TECHNICAL DEPARTMENTS

SOFTWARE DEVELOPMENT

Design

The department will design a software product's frontend. This will be the most flexible department of the club. You will be creating amazing interactive UI and UX designs. Like the ones used on this website.

Web/App Dev

Department will work to create front-end and back-end services. You will be creating websites, mobile applications, API and much more. You will be in a pool of learning that no-one has regretted diving into, a deep and ever-expanding one, full of amazing opportunities.

ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE

Applied AI

Department's main focus will be to use cutting edge AI in numerous real-world applications ranging from chatbots to art and music creation. You will be having a lot of fun working on state-of-the-art projects and gaining hands-on experience in different fields of AI

AI Research

The department focuses on DL from an intuition-first perspective where we'd focus on getting through the scary mountain of maths and implement research papers through projects — starting with the simple ones first, making the demanding and challenging tasks easier.

NON-TECHNICAL DEPARTMENTS

Content Writing

If your thoughts keep making it to people and paper, a team awaits you to become the eyes and ears of ICAS. A plan to develop critical and philosophical thinking, two tools a writer has never regretted being equipped with. You're gonna be writing articles, beholding art, and making memories!

Public Relation

The department will be handling main managerial and public relations concerns in the club. A short and effective team aimed to bring opportunities to the club.

How to contribute?

Let's use the full potential of our institution and create ripples of innovation at far corners of our reach.

  • If you want to contribute as a project head send us an email and we will reach you. Keep in mind before applying you must have a formulated project idea and sufficient skills to lead and manage a team.
  • PR members will be directly recruited by the executive board.
  • Juniors and seniors will be selected after the mini task phase.
  • Note that even though the club has departments, you'll spend your time working across all branches. The inner workings of the club will be very flexible.
  • Our aim is to mimic the workings and culture of a modern tech startup.
