The very first initiative of its kind,thrives on catapulting efforts of highly driven indinviduals towards realising their projects, that will not only prime them for universities abroad and proffessional journey but make them an essential part of making our institution a more connected community than it has ever been.

WHAT DO WE DO HERE?

Working on valuable projects:


Our club has a strong focus on project base learning, that consists of strengthening the fundamental concepts to then intuitively solve real world problems.

Working on technical and non-technical literature:


The club will have an ever expanding domain. Brain storming sessions and discussing new ideas will be a regular part of club activities. We beleive expertise over a subject with true articulation creates souvenirs of creativity. We will not just learn skills but the process of creating them and voicing them out through regular events and activities.

OUR GOAL

A novel way of savouring your days at college!

Any kind of platform,that brings together people in collaborating to achieve common landmarks of learning objectives that also coincide with their interests is always a delight to be a part of as a student. On getting a glimpse of the diversity of interests our club aims to cater to,one would realise our club's wholistic approach to learning. A diversified skillset can make learning more efficient and meaningully fun.

Promoting startup oriented interdisciplinary learning!

New ideas will be formalized and channeled into Projects, ones that will have an interdisciplinary approach aimed to make an individual not just learn development but how to gather ideas and systematically channel them into products. We will learn about different established systems and how technological innovations can create and influence one.

TECHNICAL DEPARTMENTS

SOFTWARE DEVELOPMENT

Design

The department will design a software product's frontend. This will be the most flexible department of the club. You will be beholding art and creating amazing interactive UI and UX designs, like used in this website.

Web/App Dev

Department will work to create front-end and back-end services. You will be creating websites, mobile applications, API's and much more. Expect a journey that is ever expanding an full with excitements.

ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE

AI Dev

Department's main focus will be to use cutting edge AI technology in numerous real world applications ranging from chatbots to autonomous drones to fun projects like creation of art and music. If you believe in a practical approach to things then this is the department for you.

AI Research

Department focuses on learning state of the art AI techniques and developing projects from ground up. This is the most demanding and challenging but the most innovative department of the club.

How to contribute?

Let's use the full potential of our institution and create ripples of innovation at far corners of our reach.

  • If you want to contribute as a project head send us an email and we will reach you. Keep in mind before applying you must have a formulated project idea and sufficient skills to lead and manage a team.
  • PR members will be directly recruited by the executive board.
  • Juniors and seniors will be selected after the mini task phase.
  • Note that even though the club has departments, you'll spend your time working across all branches. The inner workings of the club will be very flexible.
  • Our aim is to mimic the workings and culture of a modern tech startup.
