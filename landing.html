WHAT ARE WE DOING HERE?

Working on valuable projects:


Our club has a strong focus on project based learning, something that you will be subjected to in your further journey in this field. Projects will be focussed on learning basic concepts first and then solving real world problems.

Working on technical and non-technical literature:


The club will have an ever expanding domain. Brain storming sessions and discussing new ideas will be a regular part of club activities. We beleive expertise over a subject with true articulation creates souvenirs of creativity. We will not just learn skills but the process of creating it and voicing it out through regular events and activities.

OUR GOAL

A novel way of savouring your days at college!

Any kind of platform, that brings together people in collaborating to achieve common objectives, and landmarks of learning that coincide with their personal interests is always a delight for any student. On getting the glimpse of the sheer variety of interests our club aims to cater to through its objectives, one will come across our wholistic approach towards learning, which is to realise how fun it can be, by observing what parts of the world it influences.

Promoting startup oriented interdisciplinary learning!

New ideas will be formalised and channelised into Projects. Projects that will have an interdisciplinary approach aimed to make an individual not just learn development but how to gather ideas and systematically channelise them into products. We will learn about different established systems and how technological innovations can create and influence one.
